About This Project

WattsOwed is an independent research project by a student at Kempsville High School in Virginia Beach, VA. The goal is not to criticize AI — it is to make the real resource costs of AI infrastructure tangible and understandable for everyday Virginians who are affected through their electricity bills, water supply, and grid reliability.

If you are an educator, researcher, journalist, or advocate who wants to share or build on this data, please reach out. This site is open-source and hosted on GitHub Pages.
